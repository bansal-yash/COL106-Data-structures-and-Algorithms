 1 The addressee had written: You are eager to know about true orthodox opinion about the subject of untouchability. You can do full justice to your desire to know this if you wire to (1) His Holiness Sri Sankaracharya Swami of Kamakotipeetam, now camping at Madras, (2) His Holiness the Jeer, Sri Ahobila Matam, Srirengam, (3) His Holiness Sri Sankaracharya Swami of Sarada Peetam, Sringeri, (4) His Holiness Sri Satyagnanatirtha Swami of Uttaradimatam, now camping at Benares, requesting them to send the best pundits amongst them under their nomination for the conference you intend holding. Without this your true object cannot be fully fulfilled.
